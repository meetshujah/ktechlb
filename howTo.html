What is the e-mark ?

The e-mark is an electronic mobile marking device to create and change your own imprints by using an app on a mobile device or a PC and print them with an easy sideways movement.

How does it work?

Connect your e-mark via Wifi to your mobile device (smartphone, tablet or laptop) or by USB with your PC Create your own imprints or use existing templates Send the imprint to the e-mark … and slide
